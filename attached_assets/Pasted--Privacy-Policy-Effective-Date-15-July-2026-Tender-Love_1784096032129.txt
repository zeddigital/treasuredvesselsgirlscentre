We use your information to:

* Respond to enquiries and requests.
* Process donations and sponsorships.

## 8. Protection of Children's Privacy

Tender Love Orphan Care is committed to protecting the privacy and dignity of children.

Photographs, stories, and other content featuring children are used responsibly and, where appropriate, with consent from guardians or authorised representatives. Sensitive information that could compromise a child's safety or identity will not be published.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through our website without appropriate consent.
